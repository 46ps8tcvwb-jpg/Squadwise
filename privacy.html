Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: Your player data stays in your browser. We don't collect it, store it, or sell it. Ever.

1. What Data We Collect

Squadwise collects minimal data to operate the service:

  • Email address — collected by Stripe when you subscribe, used to manage your subscription
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe, we never see your card details
  • Subscription status — we store whether you have an active subscription

2. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Player names, stats, or match data (stored in your browser only)
  • Your Anthropic API key (stored in your browser only)
  • Coaching notes or team information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

3. Local Storage

All player data, match history, and app settings are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. Clearing your browser data will delete this information.

4. Your Anthropic API Key

When you use AI features, your Anthropic API key is stored locally in your browser and sent directly from your browser to Anthropic's servers. Squadwise servers never see or store your API key. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle your data.

5. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. When you subscribe, you are subject to Stripe's privacy policy and terms of service. Stripe may collect your email, name, and payment details. We receive only your subscription status and a customer ID from Stripe.

6. Cookies

Squadwise does not use tracking cookies. Stripe may set cookies for payment processing purposes.

7. Third Party Services

Squadwise uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic — AI analysis (governed by Anthropic's privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (governed by Stripe's privacy policy)
  • Netlify — website hosting (governed by Netlify's privacy policy)

8. Data Retention

Since we don't store your player data, there is nothing to delete on our end. Your local browser data can be cleared at any time through your browser settings. Stripe retains payment records as required by law.

9. Children's Privacy

Squadwise is intended for coaches and adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your subscription data by contacting us
  • Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Delete your local data by clearing your browser storage
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, please contact us through the app.
